Citizen Science

Citizen science (also known as crowd science, crowd-sourced science, civic science, volunteer monitoring or networked science) is scientific research conducted, in whole or in part, by amateur (or nonprofessional) scientists. Citizen science is sometimes described as "public participation in scientific research," participatory monitoring and participatory action research. (From wikipedia.org)

CrowdSourcing lens-modeling is on analyzing mass distributions of gravitational lenses by adopting citizen science. This project is inspired by an existed citizen science project on lens-finding - SpaceWarps. Hopefully, we could utilize citizen science to improve the efficiency of the analysis of gravitational lenses, which can help us to solve the big data problem in the next generation large sky surveys.
