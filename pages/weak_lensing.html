Superclusters in the Dark Energy Survey

Superclusters have been observed in the Dark Energy Survey (DES). The mass of superclusters can be very large, in the regime of 5e16 solar-masses. Using the data from a cosmological N-body simulation named the “Mira Universe”1, we are producing a simulated convergence map that represents a DES-like observation. Weak lensing peak counting and the redmapper2 galaxy finder are applied to identify superclusters — the same proce- dure as followed in the DES observation. Finally, going back to the simulation data, we can reveal the underlying structure of superclusters. This project is in progress.
