  • Simulations of Gravitational Lensing
I have been working on simulations of gravitational lensing, which includes strong and weak lensing in galaxy clusters, and galaxies. Strong lensing in galaxy clusters, our aim is to produce identical simulated images to the real observations......... Weak lensing in galaxy clusters, producing simulated shear map, then combining with strong lensing, we can test the accuracy and precision of lensing modeling. Strong galaxy-galaxy lensing, galaxy scale strong lensing is also one of my interests, it covers lensing statistics, lensing modeling, and constraining on cosmological model. Involving time delay in our strong galaxy-galaxy lensing simulations will be the next priority. Weak galaxy-galaxy lensing, I works on weak galaxy galaxy lensing as well, I am trying to use galaxy-galaxy shear and flexion to study the mass distribution and mass to light ratio of different type of galaxies. Large scale simulated convergence map can be generated using our code too. We are using the tool to study the properties of super clusters which are found in DES survey. Cluster scale CMB lensing can be used to calibrate the mass the lens clusters. But first of all we need to understand the uncertainties of some factors, such as TSZ, KSZ, and Structure on the line of sight. More is coming soon.
